Projects we successfully developed

EasyKitchen — food ordering platform

We developed the EasyKitchen online service from scratch, featuring a user-friendly dish catalog, shopping cart, and bonus system. Built with Nuxt.js and SSR for high performance and SEO, the platform also supports PWA functionality. Users earn bonus points for each order and can redeem them on future purchases, while tracking order history and bonus balance in their personal accounts.

Technologies used: Express.js with RBAC on the backend; UnoCSS, Tailwind CSS, TanStack Query, and Pinia on the frontend.

Frontend DevOps .NET Mobility

Calculation Plugin for DAT

We developed a plugin for multi-regional car service networks that calculates the cost of technical work, taking into account taxes, currencies, and business rules. The system supports filtering by job type and cost range, with task distribution between organizations either manually or via pre-set rules.

Technologies used: React, Effector, Formik, TypeScript.

MedMundus — Global platform for online medical consultations

We optimized a medical platform for booking and conducting online consultations with healthcare professionals. Our solution includes secure doctor registration with diploma verification, symptom- and tag-based filtering, convenient slot booking, and built-in video calls with file-sharing chat.

Technologies used: React, Effector, Formik, TypeScript.
